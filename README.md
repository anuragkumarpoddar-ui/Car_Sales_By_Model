# Car_Sales_By_Model

key insights:
🔹 Profit & Sales Performance
The dashboard highlights a total profit of $78.4M with over 58K units sold, indicating strong overall business performance.
🔹 Dealer-Level Insights
Profit distribution across dealer IDs shows noticeable variation, helping identify high-performing dealers and opportunities for optimization.
🔹 Model-Wise Recalls
Certain car models (like Beaufort and Labrador) show higher recall counts, which could indicate quality or manufacturing concerns that need deeper analysis.
🔹 Monthly Trends
By combining profit and quantity sold, we can observe seasonal fluctuations—useful for demand forecasting and strategic planning.
🔹 Customer Sentiment Analysis
The Tree Map visualization reveals that positive sentiment dominates, but there is still a portion of neutral and negative feedback—highlighting areas for customer experience improvement.
🔹 System-Level Recall Analysis
The heatmap provides a clear view of which car systems (like engine, brakes, etc.) are most frequently affected across models, enabling targeted issue resolution.
